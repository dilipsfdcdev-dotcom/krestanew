# PLANTATION SECTION

## Green Legacy — 27+ Plant Species

The Meadow Breeze features a carefully curated plantation of **27+ species** with **600-900 plants per plot**, creating an oxygen-rich, organic environment.

---

## Why These Plants for The Meadow Breeze?

### Strategic Plant Selection Philosophy

Our plantation strategy has been designed with specific objectives aligned to the vision of The Meadow Breeze:

#### 1. **Climate Suitability**
All selected plants are native or well-adapted to the Telangana climate, ensuring high survival rates and minimal maintenance. The Rachakonda Hills terrain with its specific soil and weather conditions has been carefully analyzed.

#### 2. **Oxygen-Rich Environment**
Plants like Peepal (24-hour oxygen release), Neem, and Banyan are selected specifically for their superior air purification capabilities, creating a pollution-free sanctuary.

#### 3. **Sustainable Ecosystem**
The combination of avenue trees, fruit plants, palms, and bamboo creates a self-sustaining ecosystem that attracts birds, butterflies, and beneficial insects.

#### 4. **Economic Value**
Fruit-bearing trees provide owners with organic produce, while timber trees like Mahogany appreciate in value over decades.

#### 5. **Aesthetic Beauty**
Flowering trees like Gulmohar, Tabebuia Rosea, and Kadamba ensure year-round color and beauty across the property.

#### 6. **Privacy & Windbreak**
Bamboo varieties and tall palms provide natural privacy screens and wind protection for farmhouses.

#### 7. **Low Maintenance**
All plants selected are drought-tolerant and require minimal care once established, supported by the drip irrigation infrastructure.
